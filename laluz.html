La Luz Hiking Trail

About

La Luz is the most beautiful hiking trail in ABQ. You can take the La Luz Trail eight miles up to the breath-taking Sandia Crest. You will climb the west face of the Sandia Mountains and see the landscape change as the altitude does.

This hike is very strenuous since the peak is 9,702 ft. Make sure you take water and if your hike will take you past sunset, bring warm clothes.

How to get the trailhead:

Take Tramway to the Juan Tabo turnoff. Follow the signs to the parking lot. Bring $3 cash to pay for parking.
